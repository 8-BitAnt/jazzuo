Yellow Ninja's Extreme Snake (2/2/2010)

Move snakes in the right path or you won't last!

You have many snakes to control in this game and the goal is to have them eat all the apples to pass each level. This may sound simple though you need to see the game to know how complex it really is. These snakes do not always cooperate!
