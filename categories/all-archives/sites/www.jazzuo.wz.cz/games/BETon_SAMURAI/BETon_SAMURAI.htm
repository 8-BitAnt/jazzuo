BETon SAMURAI

I was always fan of samurai games, this is actualy my second samurai game and its pretty good one. I made sure that samurais will move as they should. The movements are all inspired by kendo- the japanese martial arts concerning the fighting with swords-katans

Game play

choose one of the samurais who u think that will win. If the chosen one wins u get a point and contenue in guessing. If your chosen one looses then u lost. Simple isnt it. The score is simply how many times in raw u guessed corect. Mne best score is seven. U can even bet money with ur friends on the winner, or cheer ut chosen one shouting. Enjoy
