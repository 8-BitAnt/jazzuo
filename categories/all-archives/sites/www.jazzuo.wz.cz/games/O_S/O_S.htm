Onaraburu samurai

I was always fan of samurai games, this is actualy my 3rd samurai game and its pretty good one. I made sure that samurais will move as they should. The movements are all inspired by kendo- the japanese martial arts concerning the fighting with swords-katanas

Game play

how to play bet on samurai???

S- cut (u can hold the button to cut contenuesly)

 

A- guard (u can hold the button to guard contenuously)

push quicky right or left arrow button twise to make a quick step forward or back!!

for 2 player games

Player1

S-cut

A-guard

G-left

J-right

Y-jump

Player2

num-pad 0 -cut

num-pad .(del)-guard

arrowkeys for movement
